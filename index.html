Flowers We Deliver

Roses

Their petals are edible and rose water (made from soaking the petals in water) is often added to jellies or jams, or is used as flavoring in Indian
and Chinese dishes. Roses also grow a berry-shaped fruit called rose hips. The fruit can be orange, red, dark purple, or even black.

Transvaal daisy

TAlthough the gerbera appears to be a thoroughly modern flower, it was actually named in 1737 after Traugott Gerber,
a German doctor and keen botanist. The flower itself originates from South Africa, Asia, South America and Tasmania.

Tulip

Tulips are said to have different meanings based on flower color. Red tulips represent true love (no wonder tulips are the second most popular Valentine's Day flower).
White ones are a symbol of apology and forgiveness. Purple tulips are a symbol of royalty.
